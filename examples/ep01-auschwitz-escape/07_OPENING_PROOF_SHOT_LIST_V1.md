## Opening-proof rejection conditions

- Any viewer confuses Bendera with Piechowski or cannot identify the driver.
- The inner gate and outer checkpoint look like the same barrier.
- The cold open reads as four actual SS officers rather than disguised prisoners after
  the first narration line.
- Brand ident resets the tension or behaves like a separate trailer.
- Host briefing exceeds natural speech cadence or becomes a static lecture.
- The cut into 1942 includes any travel visual.
- Bendera's car access looks like luck rather than established mechanic familiarity.
- Reconstructions are not labeled consistently.
- A fabricated readable death list or execution order appears on screen.
